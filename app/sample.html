Column .span-n semantics are relative, based on 12 units in each context.

At the top level — where the parent is usually an .inner — a .span-6 column would occupy half of its width, a .span-3 a quarter etc.

When nesting, the context is the parent column. So to create a column half the width of a .span-8 parent you'd use .span-6 (6 being half the parent's 12 units)
